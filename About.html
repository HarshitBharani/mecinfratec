About Us

MEC Infra Tech is a Indian Incorporated company and locally owned. The company offers Engineering Construction, All types of Project Material Supply, Repair and Maintenance services, AMC Services, Construction Project management, Electrical and IT field services.

We provide our services to Power distribution and transmission companies, Parastatals, oil and pipeline companies, public works agencies, municipalities, commercial/residential real estate developers and hospitals. We are committed to providing the best possible expertise & service to ensure cost effective and successful execution.

We work closely with our clients to accurately interpret their dreams/visions in drawings and bring them to the desired reality with solutions to meet their needs We communicate with our clients throughout the process to keep them informed of progress and to ensure that our project keeps on schedule and within budget.
